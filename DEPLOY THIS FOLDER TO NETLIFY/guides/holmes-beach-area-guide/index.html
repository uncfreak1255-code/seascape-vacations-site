The Lively Heart of Anna Maria Island

Situated in the center of Anna Maria Island, Holmes Beach smoothly balances residential tranquility with serving as the island's commercial heart. If you want to be close to great restaurants, grocery stores, and the best public beach facilities while still enjoying a relaxed, flip-flop lifestyle, Holmes Beach is the ideal destination.

As the largest of the three island municipalities, Holmes Beach offers the widest variety of vacation rentals, from cozy beachfront condos to expansive canal-front luxury homes with private docks. Its central location means you're never more than a short bike or free trolley ride away from the historic charms of Anna Maria City to the north or the lively atmosphere of Bradenton Beach to the south.

Families especially love Holmes Beach for its renowned Manatee Public Beach, which features top-notch amenities including lifeguards, restrooms, the beloved Anna Maria Island Beach Cafe (famous for all-you-can-eat pancakes!), and accessible parking.

Holmes Beach FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Holmes Beach located?

Holmes Beach is situated right in the center of Anna Maria Island, making it exceptionally convenient for exploring the entire island. It borders the city of Anna Maria to the north and Bradenton Beach to the south.

What is Manatee Public Beach?

Manatee Public Beach is the largest and most popular beach access point in Holmes Beach. It features ample parking, restrooms, lifeguards, a playground, volleyball courts, and the famous Anna Maria Island Beach Cafe.

Are there good restaurants in Holmes Beach?

Absolutely! Holmes Beach is the commercial heart of the island and offers numerous dining options, from casual beachfront bites to fine dining. Favorites include The Beach Bistro, Hurricane Hanks, and Skinny's Place.

How do I get around Holmes Beach?

The free Anna Maria Island Trolley runs right through Holmes Beach, making it easy to travel up and down the island without a car. It's also very walkable and bike-friendly.
