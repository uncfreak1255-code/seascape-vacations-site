Waterfront Vacation Home in Bradenton with Pool, Spa & Private Dock

Dockside Dreams sits directly on the water with a private dock, bay access, and unobstructed views where dolphins and manatees visit daily. Located just 2.9 miles from Bradenton Beach and 5.6 miles from IMG Academy, this 4-bedroom home was designed from the ground up as a vacation destination — not just a place to sleep.

The heated pool and hot tub are surrounded by mature palm trees, with a turf yard, a propane grill for cookouts, outdoor dining, a 6-person lounge area, and two outdoor TVs — so you never have to leave the backyard. Inside, the open-concept living room features an 85-inch smart TV, and the fully equipped kitchen is stocked with high-end appliances, ideal for family meals, postgame dinners, or cooking for a group.

Three king bedrooms and a dedicated bunk room with twin-over-full bunks comfortably sleep 12 guests, each with their own smart TV and blackout shades. The separate gameroom — set well apart from bedrooms — features a regulation pool table, a retro arcade station, and a chess and backgammon table.

Beach chairs, a cooler, a pack-n-play, and a high chair are all included. Bring your boat to the private dock, or book a kayak or paddleboard through local outfitters just minutes away.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Dockside Dreams from the beach?

Bradenton Beach is 2.9 miles away (about 10 minutes). Cortez Beach is 3.4 miles, and Holmes Beach is 5.1 miles. Beach chairs and a cooler are included with your stay.

How far is Dockside Dreams from IMG Academy?

IMG Academy is just 5.6 miles from Dockside Dreams — about 12 minutes by car. Perfect for families with athletes attending camps, tournaments, or recruitment visits.

Can I bring my boat to Dockside Dreams?

Yes! Dockside Dreams has a private dock with direct bay access. One boat is allowed. No jet skis. Guests may not use the boat lift (owner use only). Florida boating licenses are required.

Is the pool heated?

Yes, pool heat is available for $40/night. The heater can increase water temperature 20-25°F above outdoor temps. During cooler months, 85°F is not guaranteed.

Is Dockside Dreams pet friendly?

One small dog under 30 pounds is allowed with prior host approval. The pet fee is $230 plus tax per stay. Dogs are not permitted on the artificial turf.
