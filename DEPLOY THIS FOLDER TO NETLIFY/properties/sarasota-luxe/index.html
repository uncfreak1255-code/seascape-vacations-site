Luxury Vacation Home in Downtown Sarasota with Pool & Screened Lanai

Sarasota Luxe is located right in downtown Sarasota — walking distance to premium restaurants, boutique shopping, and the city's cultural arts district. Just 3 miles from the beach, this 4-bedroom home offers the rare combination of a downtown location with full resort-style amenities, including a heated pool, hot tub, screened lanai, and outdoor kitchen.

Every detail in this home was chosen for comfort. Three king bedrooms each have their own full bathroom, Samsung smart TV, closet, and dresser. The fourth bedroom features commercial-grade triple queen bunk beds that sleep up to 6 adults — each with an 800-pound weight capacity. This is not a kids' setup. It's designed for large groups, family reunions, and multi-family getaways.

The screened-in lanai wraps around the heated pool and hot tub, with two outdoor TVs and an outdoor kitchen with a grill — perfect for Florida living year-round. Inside, the open kitchen and dining room seats 12 comfortably, and the home is stocked with everything you need for group meals.

A secure detached 2-car garage and ample driveway parking solve what's usually the biggest challenge in downtown Sarasota. Pack-n-play, high chair, and essentials are all included.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Sarasota Luxe from the beach?

Sarasota Luxe is just 3 miles from Lido Key Beach — about 10 minutes by car. Siesta Key Beach is 6 miles away. It's located centrally located in downtown Sarasota with walkable access to shops, restaurants, and nightlife.

What makes the bunk room special?

The bunk room features commercial-grade triple queen bunk beds that sleep up to 6 adults. Each bed has an 800-pound weight capacity — these are not kids' bunks. It's the most unique sleeping arrangement in the Seascape collection.

Is there parking at Sarasota Luxe?

Yes! Sarasota Luxe has a secure detached 2-car garage plus ample driveway parking — a rare find in downtown Sarasota. No street parking is permitted.

Is the pool heated?

Yes, pool heat is available for $40/day. The screened-in lanai keeps the pool and hot tub area bug-free and comfortable year-round.

Is Sarasota Luxe pet friendly?

One small dog under 30 pounds is allowed with prior host approval. The pet fee is $230 plus tax per stay. Dogs are not permitted on the turf areas.
