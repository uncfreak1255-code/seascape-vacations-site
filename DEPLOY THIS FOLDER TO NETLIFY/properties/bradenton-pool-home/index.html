Bradenton Vacation Home with Pool & Spa — Closest to IMG Academy

This spacious 3-bedroom, 3.5-bathroom home is centrally located in Bradenton — just 2.6 miles from IMG Academy, making it the closest property in the Seascape collection to campus. Whether you're in town for a tournament, training camp, or recruitment visit, this is the home that puts you minutes away.

The private backyard features a heated pool and inground spa, surrounded by a spacious outdoor grill area with seating for 10. Smart TVs are in every bedroom, and the home comes fully stocked with everything needed for a group getaway — from the fully equipped main kitchen to a separate kitchenette with its own fridge and sink.

Three bedrooms include two queen rooms and a king room, sleeping up to 10 guests across beds and a sofa bed. All three full bathrooms are recently remodeled with walk-in showers, and a convenient half-bath serves common areas. Two washers and two dryers mean you're never waiting on laundry — a standout for families and longer stays.

Beach chairs, a beach cooler, pack-n-play, high chair, kids' dinnerware, toys, and children's games are all included. WiFi runs at 250+ Mbps. This home has a short checkout list, stays of 28+ days are welcome on request.
