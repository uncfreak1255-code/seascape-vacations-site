Family Vacation Home in Bradenton with Pool, 2 Hot Tubs & Putting Green

The Oasis lives up to its name. With a private heated pool, two separate hot tubs, a putting green, and a full gameroom, this 5-bedroom home was built for groups that want more than just a place to stay. Located just 5.1 miles from IMG Academy and minutes from Anna Maria Island's best beaches, it's the largest property in the Seascape collection — comfortably sleeping up to 16 guests.

The backyard is the centerpiece: a heated pool flanked by two hot tubs — one above-ground that stays hot at all times, one in-ground that heats up in about 30 minutes. Add turf, a putting green, outdoor dining for 6, cornhole, and a propane grill, and most guests never want to go inside.

Inside, two separate living rooms (with 75" and 55" smart TVs) give everyone room to spread out. The fully equipped kitchen handles everything from quick breakfasts to large group dinners. The dedicated gameroom includes a pool table, foosball, board games, and a second fridge — perfect for late nights without disturbing the bedrooms.

Three king bedrooms, a double-full bedroom, and a bunk room with trundles that sleeps 6 mean everyone has their own space. Pack-n-play, high chair, children's dinnerware, and board games are all included for families with young kids.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is The Oasis from the beach?

Holmes Beach is 5.4 miles away, Bradenton Beach is 5.7 miles, and Cortez Beach is 6.2 miles. Beach chairs and a cooler are included with your stay.

Does The Oasis really have 2 hot tubs?

Yes! The Oasis has two separate hot tubs — one above-ground spa that stays hot at all times, and one in-ground spa that heats up in about 30 minutes. Both are included at no extra charge.

How far is The Oasis from IMG Academy?

IMG Academy is 5.1 miles from The Oasis — about 10 minutes by car. Great for families attending camps, tournaments, or recruitment visits.

Is the pool heated?

Yes, pool heat is available for $40/night. The heater can increase water temperature 20-25°F above outdoor temps. During cooler months, 85°F is not guaranteed.

Is The Oasis pet friendly?

One small dog under 30 pounds is allowed with prior host approval. The pet fee is $230 plus tax per stay. Dogs are not permitted on the artificial turf.
