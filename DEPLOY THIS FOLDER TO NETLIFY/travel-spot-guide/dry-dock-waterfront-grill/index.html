Dry Dock Waterfront Grill

Updated March 2026 • 5 min read

Dry Dock Waterfront Grill is one of Longboat Key's best-kept secrets for casual waterfront dining. Located at 412 Gulf of Mexico Drive, Longboat Key, FL 34228, this lively spot sits right on Sarasota Bay and delivers exactly what you want from a Florida waterfront restaurant: fresh seafood, cold drinks, and unbeatable water views.

Why Dry Dock Is a Local Favorite

Dry Dock strikes the perfect balance between casual and quality. The outdoor tiki bar overlooking the bay is the main attraction — grab a stool, order a rum bucket, and watch boats cruise by. The main dining room is more refined but still relaxed, and there's a spacious deck for waterfront seating.

Like Mar Vista up the road, Dry Dock has dock space for diners arriving by boat. It's part of the Longboat Key "dock and dine" circuit that makes this stretch of the Gulf Coast so special for boaters and vacationers alike.

What to Order

The ahi tuna appetizer is a standout — seared rare with a soy glaze that'll make you order a second round. The fish of the day is always reliable (ask your server what's freshest), and the lobster mac and cheese is indulgent in all the right ways. For lunch, the fish tacos and shrimp po'boy are both excellent.

The tiki bar has its own menu with lighter fare — perfect for a casual snack with your afternoon cocktail.

Hours, Parking & Practical Tips

  • Hours: Lunch and dinner daily, typically 11:30 AM – 9:00 PM
  • Parking: Dedicated lot, but it fills up at prime dinner hours. Come for lunch or early dinner.
  • By boat: Free dock space for diners on Sarasota Bay side.
  • Reservations: Accepted and recommended for dinner, especially weekends.
  • Kid-friendly: Yes — casual vibe, kids' menu available.

Insider Tips from Locals

The tiki bar is the move for lunch. Grab a seat right on the water, order the ahi tuna and a frozen drink. On Sundays they sometimes have live music at the tiki bar — check their social media for the schedule.

Where to Stay Nearby

Dry Dock is on Longboat Key, a short drive south of Anna Maria Island. Our Seascape Vacations properties are perfectly positioned for exploring both islands. Consider making it a waterfront dining day — lunch at Dry Dock, then dinner at Mar Vista just up the road.
