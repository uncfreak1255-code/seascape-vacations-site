Anna Maria Oyster Bar on the Pier

Updated March 2026 • 6 min read

Anna Maria Oyster Bar on the Pier is one of the most popular restaurants in the Bradenton area — and the pier location at 100 Highway 64, Bradenton, FL 34208 is the crown jewel of the AMOB chain. Sitting right on the Manatee River, it offers sweeping waterfront views that pair perfectly with a mountain of fresh oysters.

Why the Pier Location Stands Out

There are several Anna Maria Oyster Bar locations, but "on the Pier" is the one everyone's talking about. Built over the Manatee River, the restaurant features a massive outdoor deck that looks west over the water — which means spectacular sunsets. It's bigger than the other locations, the views are unmatched, and there's often live music on weekends.

The restaurant is a staple for both locals and visitors. On any given Friday night, you'll see families, groups of friends, and couples all enjoying the same thing: cold drinks, fresh seafood, and that waterfront breeze.

What to Order

Obviously, the oysters. AMOB sources their oysters fresh and serves them raw on the half shell, steamed, or Rockefeller-style. The steamed oysters by the dozen are the signature move — affordable, fresh, and addictive. Beyond oysters, the grouper Reuben is legendary (yes, a Reuben made with fresh grouper instead of corned beef — trust us), and the peel-and-eat shrimp is exactly what you want on a waterfront deck.

The happy hour specials are some of the best in Bradenton — discounted oysters, drafts, and well drinks. Come between 3-6 PM for the best deals.

Hours, Parking & Practical Tips

  • Hours: Open daily, typically 11:00 AM – 9:30 PM (extended hours on weekends)
  • Parking: Large free parking lot — one of the easier waterfront restaurants to park at.
  • Reservations: Accepted for larger parties. Walk-ins are fine for small groups but expect a wait on weekend evenings.
  • Kid-friendly: Absolutely — kids' menu, casual atmosphere, lots of space.
  • Live music: Weekends, usually starting around 5 PM on the outdoor deck.

Insider Tips from Locals

Tuesday is the move — they run oyster specials that make an already-good deal even better. Get the steamed dozen and the grouper Reuben. Sit on the deck, face west. You'll understand why people move to Florida.

Where to Stay Nearby

The Pier location is on the mainland in Bradenton, just a few minutes from the bridge to Anna Maria Island. It's centrally located for any of our Seascape Vacations rental properties. If you're staying in a Bradenton waterfront rental, you're probably less than 10 minutes away.
