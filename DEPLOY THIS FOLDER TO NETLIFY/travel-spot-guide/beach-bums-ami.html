Beach Bums AMI

Updated March 2026 • 4 min read

Beach Bums AMI is the go-to beach equipment rental company on Anna Maria Island. Whether you need beach chairs, umbrellas, kayaks, paddleboards, or bikes, Beach Bums delivers right to your beach spot or vacation rental — making your day at the beach completely hassle-free.

What Beach Bums Offers

Beach Bums operates on Anna Maria Island and surrounding beaches, offering delivery-based rentals that save you from hauling gear. Their core offerings include:

  • Beach chair & umbrella packages: Set up on the sand before you even arrive
  • Kayak rentals: Single and tandem kayaks, great for exploring the mangrove tunnels
  • Paddleboard (SUP) rentals: Perfect for the calm Gulf waters and bay side
  • Bike rentals: Cruiser bikes for exploring the island car-free
  • Snorkel gear, boogie boards, and more

The delivery model is what sets them apart — you tell them where you'll be (beach location or rental address), and they set up. At the end of the day, they pick everything up. No loading the car, no dragging chairs across the sand.

Pricing & Booking Tips

Pricing varies by season and package, but Beach Bums is competitively priced for the island. Multi-day rentals offer better per-day rates, and weekly packages are the best deal if you're staying for a full vacation. They typically offer online booking through their website or you can call to arrange.

Pro tip: Book your beach setup for the morning of your arrival day. You'll walk onto the beach to chairs and an umbrella already waiting — it's the kind of small luxury that makes a vacation feel premium.

Why It's Great for Vacation Rental Guests

When you're staying at a Seascape Vacations rental, you're not at a resort with a beach attendant setting up chairs for you. That's where Beach Bums fills the gap. They essentially give you the resort beach experience at your vacation rental — delivered setup, quality equipment, no hassle.

Many of our guests use Beach Bums for their entire stay. Pair it with our waterfront rentals with kayak access or SUP-friendly properties for the ultimate active beach vacation.

Practical Information

  • Service area: Anna Maria Island, Holmes Beach, Bradenton Beach
  • Booking: Online or by phone — book at least a day ahead during peak season (Jan-April)
  • Delivery: They'll deliver to your beach spot, rental address, or public beach access point
  • Hours: Typical beach hours, setups in the morning, pickups in the evening
