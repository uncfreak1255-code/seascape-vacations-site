C Ponies Beach Horseback Rides

Updated March 2026 • 5 min read

C Ponies Beach Horseback Rides offers one of the most unique and memorable experiences on the Florida Gulf Coast — horseback riding on the beach. Based near Bradenton, they lead guided rides along the shoreline of Palma Sola Bay and local beaches, giving riders an unforgettable perspective on the area's natural beauty.

What to Expect

C Ponies runs guided horseback rides that take you along the water's edge, through shallow bay waters, and along sandy stretches. The rides are suitable for all experience levels, from complete beginners to experienced riders. The horses are well-trained, calm, and accustomed to the beach environment.

Rides typically last 1-2 hours, depending on the package you choose. Sunset rides are the most popular — riding a horse along the water as the sun drops into the Gulf is genuinely a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Ride Options

  • Sunset Beach Ride: The signature experience — timed to catch the Gulf Coast sunset from horseback. Books up fast.
  • Morning Beach Ride: Cooler temperatures, fewer crowds, beautiful morning light on the water.
  • Private rides: Available for couples, proposals, or special occasions.

Booking & Practical Tips

  • Location: Palma Sola area, near Bradenton — exact meeting point given at booking
  • Reservations: Required — they do not accept walk-ups. Book well in advance during peak season (December-April).
  • Age/weight limits: Minimum age and maximum weight apply for safety — check when booking.
  • What to wear: Closed-toe shoes required. Long pants recommended. Bring sunscreen.
  • Photography: They often have a photographer who captures your ride — photos available for purchase after.

Insider Tips

Book the sunset ride at least 2 weeks in advance during winter season — it sells out fast. Wear clothes you don't mind getting a little sandy and salty. And bring a waterproof phone case because you'll want photos but you WILL get splashed.

Where to Stay Nearby

C Ponies operates near Bradenton, making it an easy add-on activity from any of our Seascape Vacations rental properties. It's one of the most popular activities our guests do — and the one they talk about most when they get home.

Pair a morning ride with an afternoon at Anna Maria Island's beaches, or end a sunset ride with dinner at the Anna Maria Oyster Bar on the Pier — which is just a short drive away.
