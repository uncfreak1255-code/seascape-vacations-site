Why Bradenton & Sarasota for Your Gulf Coast Vacation

Our homes sit between two of Florida's best-kept-secret beach towns. Bradenton puts you 10-15 minutes from Anna Maria Island — seven miles of white sand without a single high-rise blocking the view. Sarasota gives you Siesta Key and Lido Beach, consistently ranked among the country's best. Either way, you're close enough to hit both in one trip.

Unlike the Orlando corridor or Miami Beach, the Bradenton-Sarasota stretch still has that old Florida character — local restaurants where the snapper was caught that morning, kayak launches where you might be the only person on the water, and neighborhoods where kids ride bikes to the ice cream shop. It's why families keep coming back, and why our repeat booking rate keeps climbing.
