Common Questions

Need-to-Know: Bachelor Bachelorette Rentals Florida

Which property is best for a bachelor/bachelorette party?

The Oasis is our top pick for large celebration groups — it sleeps 16 with a pool, hot tub, game room, and spacious outdoor area. Sarasota Luxe works well for smaller, more upscale bachelorette groups near downtown dining and shopping.

Are there things to do for the wedding party?

Beach days, boat charters, fishing trips, spa days, golf outings, sunset cruises, bar hopping in Siesta Key Village, fine dining in Sarasota, paddleboarding, and poolside relaxation. The Gulf Coast offers activities for every type of celebration.

Is there a noise policy?

Our homes are in residential neighborhoods, so we ask that noise levels stay respectful, especially after 10 PM. Indoor celebrations, pool areas, and normal conversation are fine — just be mindful of neighbors.

Can we have a small event or gathering?

Small group gatherings are welcome! For anything beyond a normal vacation stay, please discuss your plans with us in advance. We want to make sure everything goes smoothly for you and the neighborhood.

How many people can your homes accommodate for parties?

Our homes sleep 8–16 guests overnight. For celebration gatherings, the comfortable capacity depends on the property. Contact us to discuss your group size and we'll recommend the best fit.

Are there restaurants for group dinners?

Several area restaurants accommodate large groups with private dining or group reservations. Beach Bistro, The Sandbar, Marina Jack, and numerous Sarasota restaurants can handle wedding party dinners. Book reservations well in advance.
