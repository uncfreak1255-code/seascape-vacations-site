Common Questions

Inside Scoop: Hot Tub Stays

Are hot tubs heated year-round?

Yes! Our hot tubs are maintained and heated year-round for your comfort. No matter if you're visiting in December or July, the hot tub is ready and waiting. Hot tub temperatures are kept at a comfortable 100–104°F.

Do any homes have both pool AND hot tub?

Yes! Dockside Dreams and The Oasis both feature private heated pools alongside separate hot tubs. This combination is perfect for families — kids play in the pool while adults relax in the spa.

How many people can the hot tubs accommodate?

Our hot tubs typically accommodate 4–8 adults comfortably, depending on the property. They're spacious enough for couples and small groups to enjoy together. The jets and seating are designed for genuine relaxation, not just a quick soak.

Are the hot tubs clean and well-maintained?

Absolutely. Our hot tubs are professionally serviced on a regular schedule with proper chemical balancing and cleaning. Water is treated and tested between every guest stay. We take water quality seriously so you can relax without worry.

Can I use the hot tub late at night?

Yes, our hot tubs are available for use throughout your stay, including evenings. Many guests say evening hot tub sessions under the stars are the highlight of their trip. We just ask that noise levels stay respectful after 10 PM for the neighbors.

Are the hot tubs separate from the pool?

Yes, our hot tubs are separate from the swimming pools — they're standalone spa units with their own jets, heating, and temperature controls. This means you can enjoy the hot tub at 102°F while the pool stays at a refreshing swimming temperature.
