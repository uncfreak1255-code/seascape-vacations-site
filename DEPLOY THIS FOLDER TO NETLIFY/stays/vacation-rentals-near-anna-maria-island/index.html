Need-to-Know: Near AMI Rentals

How far are your Bradenton rentals from Anna Maria Island beaches?

Our Bradenton properties are just 10–15 minutes from Anna Maria Island via the Palma Sola Causeway. The drive is scenic and easy, crossing over the bay with beautiful water views. Once on the island, free public beach access points are available at Holmes Beach, Bradenton Beach, and Coquina Beach.

Why should I stay in Bradenton instead of directly on Anna Maria Island?

Three big reasons: (1) More space — our homes have private pools, large yards, and 3–5 bedrooms vs. cramped beach condos. (2) Better value — save 40–60% on nightly rates compared to on-island properties. (3) More amenities — boat docks, hot tubs, game rooms, and outdoor entertaining areas that beachfront properties simply can't offer.

Is parking a problem on Anna Maria Island?

Parking can be challenging on AMI during peak season, especially near popular beaches. Staying in Bradenton means you have your own private parking and can time your beach trips to avoid the crowds. Early morning and late afternoon visits are ideal for easy parking and fewer crowds.

What else is there to do in Bradenton besides the beaches?

Bradenton has its own excellent attractions: Robinson Preserve for kayaking and nature walks, the Riverwalk for dining and events, Village of the Arts for galleries, South Florida Museum, IMG Academy, and a colorful food scene including fresh-off-the-boat seafood at Star Fish Company in Cortez Village.

Are your near-AMI rentals good for families?

They're ideal for families. Private pools with safety fencing, spacious yards for kids to play, multiple bedrooms so everyone sleeps well, and full kitchens for preparing meals. Many families tell us they prefer spending half the day at the beach and half at the pool — something you can only do with a private pool home.

Do you offer weekly discounts for near-AMI stays?

Yes, weekly stays receive better per-night rates than shorter bookings, and monthly stays offer even deeper discounts. Book direct at seascape-vacations.com for the best rates — you'll always pay less than Airbnb or VRBO since we don't charge platform service fees.
