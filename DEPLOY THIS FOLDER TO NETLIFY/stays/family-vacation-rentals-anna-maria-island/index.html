Anna Maria Island has earned a reputation as one of Florida's best family vacation destinations, and it's easy to see why. Unlike the mega-resort beaches of South Florida, AMI offers a laid-back, small-town atmosphere where kids can safely ride bikes, play on uncrowded beaches, and spot dolphins from the historic city pier. The island and surrounding Bradenton area are packed with family-friendly activities — from Robinson Preserve's nature trails to the Bishop Museum of Science, from mini-golf to dolphin-watching boat tours.

Seascape Vacations offers family-sized vacation homes with 3–5 bedrooms, private heated pools, game rooms, and fully stocked kitchens that make family meals easy and affordable. We're parents ourselves, and we understand what families need: safe pool fencing, enough bedrooms so everyone sleeps well, reliable WiFi for the teenagers, and a kitchen where you can prep snacks without spending $50 at a restaurant every meal. Common Questions

Straight Talk: Family AMI Rentals

Are the pools safe for children?

Yes, all our pools have proper safety fencing and gates that meet Florida code requirements. Pool areas are enclosed and private. We always recommend direct adult supervision whenever children are near the water, and we can provide additional safety information upon request.

What family activities are near Anna Maria Island?

The area is packed with family fun: Anna Maria Island beaches, Robinson Preserve nature trails, Bishop Museum of Science, Mixon Fruit Farms, mini-golf, dolphin-watching tours, kayaking, shelling on Egmont Key, the Anna Maria City Pier, and kid-friendly restaurants like the Ugly Grouper and Tide Tables.

Do you provide cribs, high chairs, and baby equipment?

Yes, cribs and high chairs are available upon request at most properties at no additional charge. For other baby equipment like strollers, pack-n-plays, and beach gear, we can recommend local rental companies that deliver directly to the property before your arrival.

Which property is best for a family with young children?

The Oasis is our top pick for families with young children — it features a large fenced yard, private heated pool with safety fence, spacious living areas, and a game room. For larger families, Dockside Dreams offers 5 bedrooms with plenty of room for everyone.

How far are your homes from the beach?

Our family homes are 10–15 minutes from Anna Maria Island's best beaches. While we're not directly beachfront, this means you get a private pool, more space, better rates, and easier parking. Many families tell us they actually prefer the pool over the beach on some days!

Is Anna Maria Island safe for families?

AMI is one of the safest vacation destinations in Florida. The island has a small-town feel with low crime, minimal traffic, and a family-oriented community. Kids can ride bikes on Pine Avenue, play in the calm Gulf waters, and explore the island safely.
