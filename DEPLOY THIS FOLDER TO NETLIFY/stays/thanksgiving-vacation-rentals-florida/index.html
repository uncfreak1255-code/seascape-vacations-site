Thanksgiving Vacation Rentals in Florida

Full kitchens, outdoor dining for 16, and pool time between courses. Thanksgiving the Florida way.

Updated March 2026

By Seascape Vacations Team | Last Updated: March 2026

Thanksgiving in a vacation rental changes the entire holiday dynamic. Instead of one person slaving in a kitchen while everyone else watches football on the couch, you've got a full kitchen where multiple cooks can work, an outdoor grill for the turkey (freeing up the oven for sides), and a screened lanai where people actually spread out and enjoy each other's company. Add a heated pool and 75°F weather, and you've transformed Thanksgiving from a stressful obligation into a genuine vacation.

Seascape Vacations properties are built for this. The Oasis fits 16 people across 5 bedrooms with a kitchen large enough for a full Thanksgiving spread, two dining areas (indoor and outdoor), and enough outdoor space that the kids burn off energy instead of bouncing off walls. Dockside Dreams and River House each sleep 12 with waterfront views that make the holiday feel special without anyone doing extra decorating.

Cooking Thanksgiving Dinner at the Rental

The oven situation: Every property has a full-size oven that handles a 20-lb turkey. But here's the Florida move — grill or smoke the turkey outdoors on the gas grill or bring a portable smoker. Grilled turkey is juicier than oven-roasted (the moisture stays in), and it frees the oven entirely for casseroles, pies, and rolls. Our guests who've tried this say they'll never go back to oven turkey.

Publix does the heavy lifting: Publix's Thanksgiving meal deals are legendary in Florida. Order a fully cooked Thanksgiving dinner for 10-14 people ($50-80) — turkey, stuffing, mashed potatoes, cranberry sauce, gravy, rolls, and a pumpkin pie. Pick it up the day before, reheat at the rental, and add your family's signature dishes. Nobody spends the whole day cooking, everyone eats well, and you spent less on the entire dinner than one restaurant meal.

For serious cooks: Detwiler's Farm Market on 14th Street has incredible fresh produce, organic turkeys (order ahead), artisan bread, and specialty ingredients. The Bradenton Farmers' Market at Riverwalk runs Saturday mornings — hit it the weekend before Thanksgiving for local produce, pies from local bakers, and the best garlic bread you've ever had.

The Day-After-Thanksgiving Tradition: Beach Day

While the rest of America fights Black Friday crowds, you're on Anna Maria Island. The Friday after Thanksgiving has some of the most pleasant beach weather of the year — mid-70s, low humidity, uncrowded sand. Pack leftover turkey sandwiches, grab some chairs, and spend the day doing absolutely nothing productive. This becomes the moment everyone remembers more than the actual dinner.

Why Thanksgiving Rentals Book Fast

Thanksgiving week is our second-busiest booking period after Christmas. Families who've done one Florida Thanksgiving tend to make it annual — and they book the same property a year in advance. The combination of warm weather, pool access, full kitchen, and enough space for the whole family creates a holiday experience that's genuinely better than hosting at home.

The math works too: a Thanksgiving gathering at home means the host family does all the work — cooking, cleaning, hosting. At a rental, the workload distributes naturally because everyone's contributing to meals, and the professional cleaning between guests means you leave without scrubbing toilets. The hosting family finally gets a real vacation during the holiday instead of running a bed and breakfast for relatives.

Booking Tip: Thanksgiving week (typically Wed-Sun or Sat-Sat) books 2-4 months in advance. Contact us by August for the best property options. Many families extend to a full week (Saturday to Saturday) for the best rate — the extra days at the beach are worth it.

Keeping Everyone Happy (It's Easier Here)

The secret weapon of a vacation rental Thanksgiving is space and activities. Uncle Steve wants to fish? He's on the dock at 6 AM, happy. The teenagers want to be left alone? They've got the game room. The 6-year-olds are burning energy in the pool while adults cook and catch up on the lanai. Grandma wants quiet? She's got her own bedroom with a door that closes. Nobody's on top of each other, nobody's bored, and the family dynamics that make holidays tense at home dissolve when everyone has room to breathe.

Common Questions

Thanksgiving Trip Questions

Do the properties have full kitchens for Thanksgiving cooking?

Yes — every Seascape Vacations property has a full kitchen with standard oven (fits a 20-lb turkey), multiple burner stovetop, microwave, dishwasher, full-size refrigerator, and ample counter space. Kitchens are stocked with pots, pans, baking sheets, serving dishes, and utensils. The outdoor gas grill is perfect for turkey or sides.

How many people can fit for Thanksgiving dinner?

The Oasis seats 16 with indoor and outdoor dining areas. Dockside Dreams, River House, and Sarasota Luxe each seat 10-12. All properties have outdoor dining on the screened lanai, which most groups prefer for Thanksgiving — eating outside in 72°F weather is the whole point of a Florida holiday.

What's the weather like for Thanksgiving in Bradenton?

Thanksgiving week averages 72-76°F highs and 55-60°F lows with mostly sunny skies. November is dry season — rain is uncommon. Pool water is heated to 82-86°F. It's warm enough for beach trips, outdoor dining, and pool time but cooler than summer. Evenings may need a light jacket.

Where can I buy a pre-made Thanksgiving dinner?

Publix offers complete Thanksgiving dinners for 10-14 people ($50-80) — order in advance and pick up the day before. Detwiler's Farm Market has organic turkeys, fresh produce, and specialty items. Most grocery stores close early on Thanksgiving Day, so shop by Wednesday afternoon.

How far in advance should I book for Thanksgiving?

Thanksgiving week typically books 2-4 months ahead. We recommend contacting us by August for the best property selection. Many families book Saturday-to-Saturday to get the best rate and enjoy extra beach days before and after the holiday.
