Why Rent a Beach House Instead of a Hotel?

Hotels on Anna Maria Island run $300-500/night for a single room. You get a bed, a mini fridge, and a pool you share with 200 other guests. For the same price — or less — our beach houses give you 4-5 bedrooms, a private heated pool, a full kitchen, laundry, and a yard where the kids can run around without you worrying about noise complaints.

The math gets even better with a group. Split a $400/night beach house four ways and you're paying $100/night per couple — with infinitely more space and privacy than any hotel room. Cook breakfast at home, pack a cooler for the beach, grill dinner by the pool. You'll spend less eating out and more time actually enjoying your vacation.

And honestly? The best part is coming back from the beach and jumping into your own pool. No towel wars. No saving chairs at 6 AM. Just your crew, cold drinks, and zero strangers.

The Bradenton Advantage

Here's what most visitors don't realize until they've already booked an overpriced condo on AMI: Bradenton is right there. Twelve minutes across the Palma Sola Causeway and you're on Bradenton Beach. The sand is the same. The water is the same. The sunsets are the same.

What's different? The houses. On Anna Maria Island, $500/night gets you a 2-bedroom cottage with no pool and street parking. In Bradenton, that same budget gets you a 4-bedroom home with a heated pool, hot tub, game room, and a two-car garage. It's not even close.

Plus you're closer to everything on the mainland — Publix and Winn-Dixie for groceries, Target for anything you forgot, and actual restaurants that locals go to. Duffy's Tavern for a cold beer and a burger. Ortygia for date-night Italian. The Riverwalk for morning runs or evening strolls. Anna Maria has its charm, but Bradenton is where you actually live during your trip.

What Guests Ask Before Booking

Are your Bradenton beach houses actually close to the beach?

Our homes are 10-20 minutes from the best Gulf beaches. Bradenton Beach and Coquina Beach are about 12-14 minutes away via Manatee Avenue. Holmes Beach is 15 minutes. You're not on the sand, but you get a full-size house with a private pool and yard for a fraction of beachfront condo prices. Most of our guests tell us they prefer it this way — beach for the day, pool and grill for the evening.

What's the cancellation policy for booking direct?

We offer flexible cancellation on most bookings. Cancel 30+ days before check-in for a full refund. 14-29 days out, you get a 50% refund. Under 14 days is non-refundable unless we can rebook the dates. Specific policies vary by property and season — we'll confirm the exact terms when you book.

Do you allow events or parties at the homes?

Small gatherings are totally fine — birthday dinners, family cookouts, game nights around the pool. Large parties with outside guests, DJs, or event rentals aren't permitted. Our homes are in residential neighborhoods, and we take our neighbor relationships seriously. Planning something special? Just give us a call and we'll work with you.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

Most of our properties have a 3-night minimum during regular season and a 5-7 night minimum during peak season (December through April and major holidays). Off-season, we sometimes accept 2-night stays depending on availability. Check the calendar on each property page for current minimums.

What's included — linens, towels, kitchen essentials?

Everything you need is already there. Fresh linens, bath towels, pool towels, a fully stocked kitchen with pots, pans, dishes, and utensils. We also provide laundry detergent, dish soap, paper towels, trash bags, and a starter supply of toiletries. Just bring your clothes and groceries — Publix is 5 minutes from every property.

Can I get a discount for longer stays?

Yes. Weekly stays (7+ nights) automatically get a discount, and monthly stays get an even bigger break. The exact percentage depends on the property and season, but you're typically looking at 10-20% off the nightly rate for weekly bookings and 25-40% off for monthly. Contact us directly for a custom quote — we're almost always able to beat whatever you see on Airbnb or VRBO.
