Common Questions

Beach House Vacation Intel

Are your homes directly on the beach?

Our homes are located 10–20 minutes from the Gulf Coast's best beaches in residential areas of Bradenton and Sarasota. While not directly beachfront, this means you enjoy private pools, spacious yards, and significantly lower rates. Most guests tell us they prefer having a private pool AND beach access versus a cramped beachfront condo.

Which beaches are closest to your rental homes?

Depending on the property: Anna Maria Island beaches (Holmes Beach, Bradenton Beach, Coquina Beach) are 10–15 minutes from our Bradenton homes. Siesta Key Beach, Lido Beach, and Longboat Key are 15–25 minutes from our Sarasota property. You can easily visit a different superb beach every day.

Is the beach better than having a private pool?

Why not both? Our guests spend mornings at the beach and afternoons at their private pool — or vice versa. The pool is especially great for families with young children who need a break from waves and sand, and for evenings when you want to swim without driving anywhere.

Do you provide beach gear and chairs?

We provide recommendations for beach gear rental services that deliver chairs, umbrellas, coolers, and beach toys directly to your spot on the sand. Some properties include basic beach items like towels and a cooler. Ask us about current beach equipment availability for your specific property.

What makes the Gulf Coast beaches special?

The Gulf Coast offers calm, warm waters with gentle waves (unlike the Atlantic's rougher surf), powdery white sand (Siesta Key's quartz crystal sand stays cool in summer), spectacular sunsets over the water, and a more relaxed atmosphere than South Florida's crowded beaches. It's Florida beach living at its finest.

Are the beaches family-friendly?

Gulf Coast beaches are among the most family-friendly in Florida. The calm, shallow waters are perfect for young swimmers, the sand is clean and soft, lifeguards are on duty at main beaches, and the laid-back atmosphere means families feel welcome everywhere. Coquina Beach and Holmes Beach are particular family favorites.
