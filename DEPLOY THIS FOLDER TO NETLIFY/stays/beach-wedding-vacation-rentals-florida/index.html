Common Questions

Wedding Rental Basics

Can I use the rental for wedding prep?

Yes! Our spacious homes are perfect for bridal party preparation — multiple bedrooms for getting ready, large bathrooms with good lighting, and living areas for the wedding party to gather before the ceremony.

Where can I have a beach wedding nearby?

Anna Maria Island, Bean Point, Coquina Beach, and Lido Beach are popular ceremony locations. Many beach wedding planners and officiants operate in the area. Beach permits may be required — local planners handle all logistics.

Can I host the reception at the rental?

Our pool and outdoor areas work beautifully for intimate receptions. For larger events, please discuss your plans with us in advance. Many couples use the rental for the after-party and host the formal reception at a local venue.

How many wedding guests can you accommodate overnight?

Our properties sleep 8–16 guests. For larger wedding parties, we can coordinate multiple nearby rentals so your guests stay close together with their own space.

What wedding vendors do you recommend?

The AMI–Sarasota area has excellent wedding vendors including planners, photographers, florists, caterers, officiants, and musicians. We can provide recommendations based on your style and budget.

When is the best time for a Gulf Coast beach wedding?

October through May offers the best weather for outdoor ceremonies — pleasant temperatures, low humidity, and beautiful sunset timing. Late afternoon ceremonies catch golden hour light beautifully.
