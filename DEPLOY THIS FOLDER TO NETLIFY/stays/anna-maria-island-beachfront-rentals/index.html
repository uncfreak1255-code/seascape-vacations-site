Anna Maria Island has 7 miles of white sand Gulf beaches and zero high-rise buildings. That combination doesn't exist anywhere else in Florida. The island's strict building codes cap everything at three stories, which means unobstructed sunset views and a beach experience that feels decades removed from Miami or Clearwater.

Seascape Vacations offers luxury homes within a 12-25 minute drive of AMI's best beaches. Our properties are based in Bradenton and Sarasota — close enough for daily beach trips, but with the space, privacy, and heated pools that on-island rentals at this price point simply can't match. You'll get 4-5 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and a private backyard with a pool instead of a cramped beachfront condo.

AMI's Best Beaches — A Local's Ranking

Bean Point at the north tip is the most photogenic. The sand curves to a narrow point where Tampa Bay meets the Gulf, and at low tide you can walk 100 yards out in ankle-deep water. It's a 20-minute drive from our Bradenton properties. Downside: no facilities, limited parking on North Shore Drive.

Manatee Public Beach in Holmes Beach is the family pick. Restrooms, a food concession, lifeguards on duty during season, and the widest stretch of sand on the island. Parking fills up by 10 AM on weekends from November through April — our guests who arrive early never have trouble.

Coquina Beach at the south end is the local favorite. It's quieter, has a bayside picnic area with grills, and the Coquina Trolley Stop makes it easy to get to without a car. The snorkeling off the south jetty isn't world-class, but you'll see plenty of fish and the occasional sea turtle.

On-Island vs. Off-Island Stays — The Real Math

A 2-bedroom beachfront condo on AMI runs $350-$500/night during season. You get a small kitchen, shared pool, and a view. For that same price range with Seascape, you get a 4-5 bedroom private home with your own heated pool, spa, full gourmet kitchen, game room, and outdoor living space that fits your entire group. The tradeoff? A 12-15 minute drive to the beach.

Our guests consistently tell us the drive is worth it. You're trading a balcony view for a backyard oasis. And when everyone else on the island is fighting for parking at 11 AM, you're floating in your private pool with a drink in your hand. Different vacation experience entirely.

Beyond the Beach — What Most Visitors Miss

The Rod & Reel Pier in Anna Maria City is worth the trip even if you don't fish. Grab a bucket of steamed shrimp from the restaurant, sit on the pier, and watch pelicans dive-bomb baitfish while the sun drops. It's one of those "this is why we came to Florida" moments.

Cortez Village — the working fishing village just south of AMI — is the most underrated spot in the area. The Florida Maritime Museum is free, the stone crab at Tide Tables is fresh off the boat, and the whole place feels like Old Florida before Instagram discovered it. A 10-minute drive from our Bradenton homes.

For a full day off-island, the Sarasota area is 30 minutes south and has the Ringling Museum, St. Armands Circle shopping, and Siesta Key — which regularly gets voted America's best beach. Our Sarasota Luxe property puts you right in the middle of it all.

AMI Beachfront Rental Questions

Are your rentals actually beachfront on Anna Maria Island?

Our properties are located in Bradenton and Sarasota, 12-25 minutes from Anna Maria Island beaches. They are not directly on the beach, but you get significantly more space, private heated pools, and lower rates compared to on-island beachfront condos. Most guests tell us the short drive is well worth the tradeoff.

Which AMI beach is closest to your vacation rentals?

Coquina Beach and Manatee Public Beach in Holmes Beach are both 12-15 minutes from our Bradenton properties. Bean Point at the north tip is about 20 minutes. Our Sarasota Luxe property is 25 minutes from Coquina Beach but only 15 minutes from Siesta Key.

What is the best beach on Anna Maria Island for families?

Manatee Public Beach in Holmes Beach is the best for families. It has lifeguards, restrooms, showers, a snack concession, and the widest sand area on the island. The water is calm and shallow, and the free trolley stops right there. Coquina Beach is a close second with more shade trees and picnic facilities.

How do AMI rental prices compare to your off-island homes?

A 2-bedroom beachfront condo on AMI runs $350-$500/night. For the same price, Seascape Vacations offers 4-5 bedroom private homes with heated pools, spas, full kitchens, and outdoor entertaining spaces. You get 2-3x the space and a private pool for similar or lower nightly rates.

Is there public transportation to Anna Maria Island?

Yes. The free Anna Maria Island Trolley runs every 20 minutes during peak season. However, getting from the mainland to the island requires a car. From our Bradenton properties, it is a quick 12-15 minute drive across the Cortez or Manatee Avenue bridges.
