Common Questions

Inside Scoop: Sarasota Pool Stays

How far is Siesta Key Beach from your Sarasota pool homes?

Approximately 15–20 minutes by car. The drive is easy and scenic, taking you across the Siesta Key Bridge with beautiful bay views. Free public beach parking is available, though arriving before 10 AM during peak season ensures the best spots.

What cultural attractions are near your Sarasota rentals?

Sarasota is Florida's Cultural Coast: the Ringling Museum of Art (66-acre estate with circus museum), Marie Selby Botanical Gardens, Van Wezel Performing Arts Hall, Sarasota Opera, Florida Studio Theatre, and dozens of galleries in the downtown arts district — all within a short drive.

Are the pools heated for winter swimming?

Yes! All our Sarasota pools are heated year-round. Pool heating is included in your rental rate at no additional charge. Even during the cooler winter months (December–February), the pool water stays at a comfortable swimming temperature.

Is Sarasota Luxe good for a girls' trip or couples retreat?

Sarasota Luxe is perfect for both. The sophisticated downtown location, designer interiors, and proximity to upscale shopping at St. Armands Circle and fine dining make it ideal for a girls' weekend or romantic couples retreat. The private pool and patio add to the retreat atmosphere.

What restaurants are near your Sarasota vacation rentals?

Sarasota's dining scene is exceptional: Marina Jack for waterfront dining, Indigenous for farm-to-table, Bijou Café for French-Southern fusion, Owen's Fish Camp for creative seafood, and the entirety of St. Armands Circle's restaurant row. Plus Burns Court has charming cafés and eateries.

Can I walk to anything from your Sarasota property?

The Sarasota Luxe location provides easy access to nearby shops, restaurants, and attractions by car. While it's not in a walkable downtown core, the short drives to Sarasota's best experiences are quick and convenient, and you'll appreciate having a private, quiet home base.
