Common Questions

Planning an Anniversary Trip? Read This

Which property is most romantic for an anniversary?

River House for waterfront serenity and canal-front sunsets, or Sarasota Luxe for sophisticated living near fine dining and cultural attractions. Both feature private pools and hot tubs.

Can you help arrange anniversary surprises?

We can recommend local vendors for flowers, champagne, private chef services, sunset sailing cruises, and spa treatments. Let us know your plans and we'll connect you with the right people.

What romantic restaurants are nearby?

Beach Bistro for seriously good beachfront dining, Euphemia Haye for an incredible dessert room, Bijou Café for French-Southern fusion, and Marina Jack for waterfront sunset dinner — all perfect for anniversary celebrations.

Is the area good for a second honeymoon?

Absolutely! The Gulf Coast offers everything for a romantic reconnection: beautiful beaches, private pools, excellent dining, cultural experiences, and a pace of life that encourages slowing down together.

Do you offer special anniversary rates?

Our direct booking rates save 10–15% versus Airbnb and VRBO. Extended stays and off-peak dates offer additional value. Contact us to discuss your anniversary dates for the best available pricing.

What romantic activities can we do?

Sunset sailing, couples massages, dolphin-watching tours, private beach walks, Ringling Museum visits, wine bar evenings, kayaking, and simply relaxing together by the pool. The Gulf Coast is made for romance.
