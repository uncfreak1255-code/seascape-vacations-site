AMI Romantic Vacation Rentals for Couples

Anna Maria Island is made for romance. Sunsets, private pools, and Gulf breezes.

✍️ By Seascape Vacations Team | Last Updated: 2026-03-02

There's something about the Gulf Coast's golden sunsets, warm salt air, and unhurried pace that makes Anna Maria Island one of Florida's most romantic destinations. Unlike busy resort areas, AMI and the surrounding Bradenton–Sarasota area offer intimate experiences — sunset sailing cruises, candlelit dinners at waterfront restaurants, morning walks on quiet stretches of beach, and the kind of genuine relaxation that rekindles connection.

Seascape Vacations offers romantic vacation rental homes that set the stage for incredible couples experiences. Picture yourself relaxing in a private heated pool as the sun sets, soaking in a hot tub under the stars, or sipping wine on a waterfront deck while dolphins play in the canal. Our homes provide the privacy and atmosphere that hotels can't replicate — no shared walls, no crowded pools, no lobby noise.

For romantic dining, the area delivers: Beach Bistro for fine dining on the sand, The Sandbar for sunset cocktails, Euphemia Haye for a legendary dessert room, and dozens of charming local spots in between. Add couples massages, sailing charters, and Sarasota's cultural scene, and you have the ingredients for a truly special getaway. Book direct and save 10–15%.

Common Questions

Common Romantic Getaway Anna Maria Island Questions

Which property is best for a romantic couples getaway?

For waterfront romance, River House offers serene canal-front sunsets and a peaceful atmosphere. For a more upscale experience, Sarasota Luxe provides designer interiors near Sarasota's fine dining scene. Both feature private heated pools and hot tubs — perfect for couples.

What are the best romantic restaurants near Anna Maria Island?

Beach Bistro on Holmes Beach for exceptional beachfront fine dining, The Sandbar for sunset cocktails and seafood, Euphemia Haye on Longboat Key for an memorable dessert room, and Mar Vista Dockside for casual waterfront dining. In Sarasota, try Bijou Café or Indigenous for a special evening.

What romantic activities are available in the area?

Sunset sailing cruises, couples spa treatments, dolphin-watching boat tours, kayaking through mangrove tunnels, wine tasting, evening strolls on Anna Maria Island's Pine Avenue, Sarasota opera and theater, and private beach bonfires. We can help arrange any of these experiences.

Do any of your homes have hot tubs for couples?

Yes! Several of our properties feature private hot tubs alongside heated pools. There's nothing quite like soaking in a hot tub under the stars after a day at the beach. The hot tubs are maintained and heated year-round for your enjoyment.

Is Anna Maria Island good for a honeymoon?

AMI is a wonderful honeymoon destination. The island's laid-back charm, beautiful sunsets, and romantic dining options create the perfect honeymoon atmosphere. Our private pool homes offer the seclusion and luxury newlyweds deserve, and the area's activities provide plenty of adventure too.

Can you help arrange special touches for anniversaries or proposals?

Absolutely! While we don't provide these services directly, we can recommend local vendors for flower arrangements, private chef dinners, champagne delivery, sunset boat charters, and even beach proposal setups. Just let us know what you're planning and we'll help make it special.
