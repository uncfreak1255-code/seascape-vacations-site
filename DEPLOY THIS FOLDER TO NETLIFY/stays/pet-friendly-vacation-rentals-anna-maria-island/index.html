Finding a pet-friendly vacation rental near Anna Maria Island that isn't a cramped condo with a $200 pet deposit? That's the real challenge. Seascape Vacations manages 5 homes in Bradenton and Sarasota — all with fenced or enclosed yards, private pools (with pool safety covers available), and no ridiculous pet fees. Your dog gets a vacation too.

Here's the thing about AMI and dogs: the island itself is pretty strict. Most public beaches don't allow pets at all. But the mainland? Totally different story. Palma Sola Causeway is 8 minutes from our rentals and dogs are welcome all day — the shallow bay water is perfect for pups who love to wade. Robinson Preserve has miles of mangrove trails. And GT Bray Park has a legit off-leash dog park with water fountains and shade, just 5 minutes from The Oasis. Coquina Beach on the south end of AMI does allow leashed dogs before 9am if you're an early riser.

One of our guests brought two golden retrievers last summer. They spent mornings at the Palma Sola Causeway — the dogs loved the shallow bay water — then came back to The Oasis where the pups napped by the pool while the family played mini golf. Their exact words in the review: "Best dog-friendly rental we've ever stayed at." That's what we're going for. Big yards, real space, and a location that actually works for dog owners.

Dog Owner Q&A

What Dog Owners Ask Before Booking

Which of your properties allow dogs?

The Oasis is our go-to dog-friendly property. It has a large fenced yard, a private pool area, and 5 bedrooms that sleep 16 — so there's room for the whole crew plus your pup. The yard is big enough for fetch. We're expanding pet-friendly options across our other properties, so reach out and ask about current availability.

Is there a pet fee or pet deposit?

A small pet cleaning fee covers the deep clean after your stay. No massive deposits, no surprise charges. We tell you the exact amount when you book — zero surprises at checkout. It's significantly less than what most vacation rentals on AMI charge for pets.

Are there dog-friendly beaches on Anna Maria Island?

Honestly? AMI proper is strict about dogs on the beach. Most public beaches don't allow them. Bayfront Park on the north end permits leashed dogs, and Coquina Beach allows leashed dogs before 9am. But the real move is Palma Sola Causeway — 8 minutes from our rentals, dogs welcome all day, calm shallow bay water. Your dog will like it better than the Gulf side anyway.

What size dogs do you allow?

No blanket breed or weight restrictions. We've hosted golden retrievers, Great Danes, bulldogs, doodles of every size — basically everything. We evaluate each pet individually. Just let us know your dog's breed, size, and temperament when you book. Well-behaved dogs are what matters, not a number on a scale.

Are the yards fenced?

The Oasis has a fully fenced yard where your dog can go off-leash. The fence is solid — we haven't had any escape artists succeed yet. If you have a very small dog or a known Houdini, take a quick walk around the fence line when you arrive to make sure you're comfortable with it. Most guests say it's more than adequate.

Where's the nearest vet or pet store to your rentals?

VCA Manatee Animal Hospital is about 10 minutes away for anything serious. The Animal ER of Bradenton is open 24/7 for after-hours emergencies. For supplies, Petco on Cortez Road is 7 minutes out, and there's a PetSmart on SR 64 too. You won't have trouble finding dog food, treats, or a replacement leash if needed.
