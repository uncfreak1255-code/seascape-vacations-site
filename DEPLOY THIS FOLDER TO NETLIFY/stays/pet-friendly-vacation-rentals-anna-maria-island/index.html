Leaving your dog behind for a week is the worst part of any trip. The guilt, the sad eyes at drop-off, the kennel fees that somehow cost more than your rental car. We get it — that’s why all of our vacation homes near Anna Maria Island welcome dogs. Fenced yards, big pools (fair warning: your dog will try to get in), and enough outdoor space that your pup won’t be stuck in some tiny pet-tolerant hotel room. These are real houses with real backyards.

Here’s the honest truth about Anna Maria Island and dogs: the beaches technically don’t allow them. It’s a Manatee County ordinance, and during season the code enforcement folks are out there. But locals know the workarounds. Early morning walks before 8 AM are usually fine. Bayfront Park in Anna Maria City is officially dog-friendly. De Soto National Memorial has waterfront trails your dog will go nuts for. And the Palma Sola Causeway — right on the way to AMI — has a dog-friendly shoreline area where people bring their dogs daily. Plus, your backyard pool is basically a private dog park with a pool attached.

Practical stuff you’ll want to know: Pet Supermarket on Cortez Road is the closest pet supply store (10 minutes from most properties). Animal ER of Bradenton on Manatee Avenue is the 24-hour emergency vet — save that number before you need it. The Humane Society of Manatee County does adoption events if you’re the type of person who goes on vacation and comes home with a second dog. (No judgment. It happens.)

Dog-Friendly Spots Near Anna Maria Island

GT Bray Park Dog Park

Off-leash fenced area with separate sections for small dogs and large dogs. Benches, shade, and water stations. About 10 minutes from most of our properties. This is where Bradenton dog owners go — your pup will make friends fast.

Robinson Preserve

682 acres of nature preserve with paved and shell trails, kayak launch, and observation towers. Leashed dogs welcome everywhere. Great for a morning walk before the Florida heat kicks in. The mangrove tunnels on the kayak trail are worth seeing even without your dog.

De Soto National Memorial

Waterfront trails along Tampa Bay where leashed dogs are allowed. Free admission. The half-mile nature trail loops through mangroves to a beach overlook. Perfect early morning walk — you’ll usually have it mostly to yourself.

Palma Sola Causeway

Dog-friendly shoreline area right on the bridge road to Anna Maria Island. Shallow water, sandy patches, and plenty of room for dogs to splash around on a leash. Locals bring their dogs here almost every evening. Stop on the way to or from AMI and let your pup burn some energy.

Brohard Beach — Venice, FL

The closest true “dogs allowed on the beach” beach. About 45 minutes south on I-75. Worth the drive if your dog lives for sand and waves. Off-leash, open beach, and there’s a paw wash station at the entrance. Venice itself is a great day trip — the downtown has solid restaurants and you can hunt for shark teeth on nearby beaches.

Common Questions

Pet-Friendly Vacation Rental FAQs

Are dogs allowed at your vacation rentals near Anna Maria Island?

Yes — our properties welcome well-behaved dogs. Most homes have fenced yards with plenty of outdoor space for your pup to run around. The Oasis has the biggest fully fenced yard, and River House has a large fenced area along the canal. We do ask that guests confirm pet policy details and any applicable pet deposit when booking, since requirements can vary by property.

Can I bring my dog to Anna Maria Island beaches?

Technically, dogs are not allowed on Anna Maria Island beaches under Manatee County ordinance. But there are solid alternatives nearby: Bayfront Park in Anna Maria City is dog-friendly, De Soto National Memorial has waterfront trails where leashed dogs are welcome, and the Palma Sola Causeway has a dog-friendly shoreline area. If you want a true dog beach, Brohard Beach in Venice (about 45 minutes south) is the closest one that officially allows dogs.

Which of your properties is best for dogs?

The Oasis has the biggest yard and it’s fully fenced — dogs love it there. River House has a large fenced yard along the canal, great for dogs who like watching boats go by. Dockside Dreams has a yard but the dock area needs supervision with dogs near the water. Sarasota Luxe has a fenced yard in a quiet neighborhood near dog-friendly parks. All properties have outdoor space dogs will enjoy.

Are there dog parks near your Bradenton vacation homes?

GT Bray Park has an off-leash dog park with separate areas for small dogs and large dogs — it’s about 10 minutes from most of our properties. Lakewood Ranch has a nice dog park about 20 minutes away. Robinson Preserve (682 acres of nature trails) is great for leashed walks and is a favorite among our guests with dogs.

What pet-friendly restaurants are near Anna Maria Island?

Most outdoor dining spots in the area welcome dogs at their patios. Anna Maria Oyster Bar (Cortez Road location) is a local favorite with a big outdoor area. The Sandbar on AMI has a dog-friendly patio right on the beach. Duffy’s Tavern and Wicked Cantina in Bradenton both welcome dogs outside. Most beachfront restaurants on the island have outdoor seating where dogs are fine.

Is there a pet fee for your vacation rentals?

Pet policies and fees vary by property. We recommend contacting us when booking to confirm pet-friendliness for your specific dates and any applicable deposit. We want to make sure both you and your dog have a great stay — just let us know you’re bringing a furry friend when you reach out.
