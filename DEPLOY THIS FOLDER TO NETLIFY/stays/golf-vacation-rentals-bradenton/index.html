Common Questions

Quick Answers: Golf Stays

What golf courses are near your Bradenton vacation rentals?

Several excellent courses are within 15–20 minutes: River Run Golf Links, Legacy Golf Club at Lakewood Ranch, Waterlefe Golf & River Club, Peridia Golf & Country Club, Rosedale Golf & Country Club, and IMG Academy's golf facilities. In Sarasota, Bobby Jones Golf Club and University Park Country Club are about 25 minutes away.

Can I play golf year-round in Bradenton?

Absolutely! Bradenton's subtropical climate makes golf a year-round activity. Winter months (December–March) offer the most pleasant conditions with temperatures in the 70s. Summer rounds are warm but playable, especially with early tee times. Courses stay green and lush all year.

Are there golf packages or tee time services available?

While we don't book tee times directly, we can recommend local tee time services and golf booking apps that offer discounted rates at area courses. Many courses offer twilight rates and seasonal specials. Our local knowledge can help you pick the right courses for your skill level and budget.

Is IMG Academy open to the public for golf?

IMG Academy in Bradenton is primarily a training facility for elite athletes, but they offer some public access to their golf programs and facilities. Contact IMG directly for current public access options. The Legacy Golf Club at nearby Lakewood Ranch is a popular public alternative.

Are your homes suitable for a golf group trip?

Our homes are perfect for golf groups. Properties sleeping 10–16 guests mean your foursome (or multiple foursomes) can stay together under one roof. Private pools provide great post-round relaxation, and full kitchens make group dinners easy. The Oasis and Dockside Dreams are favorites for golf groups.

What other activities can non-golfers enjoy while the group plays?

Non-golfers have plenty to do: Anna Maria Island beaches (10–15 minutes), shopping at Ellenton Premium Outlets or St. Armands Circle, Ringling Museum, Marie Selby Gardens, spa treatments, kayaking, boat tours, and of course relaxing at the private pool. Everyone stays happy — on and off the course.
