Common Questions

Helpful Info for Large Group Trips

What's the largest group you can accommodate?

Our largest property, The Oasis, sleeps up to 16 guests across 5 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms. Dockside Dreams also sleeps 14+ guests. For groups larger than 16, we can help coordinate multiple properties in the same neighborhood so your group stays close together.

Are the homes suitable for family reunions?

Our homes are perfect for family reunions. Open floor plans with large living rooms, dining areas that seat the whole group, gourmet kitchens, private pools with spacious decks, BBQ grills, and outdoor entertaining areas. The Oasis also has a game room that kids and adults love.

Is there enough parking for a large group?

Yes, our large group rentals include ample driveway and garage parking for 3–4+ vehicles. Properties like The Oasis have extra-wide driveways. If your group has more vehicles, street parking is also available in our residential neighborhoods.

Can we cook for the whole group?

Absolutely! Our kitchens are fully equipped with full-size appliances, cookware, dishes, and utensils for large-group meal prep. The Oasis features a gourmet kitchen with double ovens, a large island, and enough dinnerware for 16 guests. Outdoor grills are perfect for BBQ nights.

What group activities are available near Bradenton?

Group favorites include: beach days on Anna Maria Island, fishing charters, dolphin boat tours, kayaking at Robinson Preserve, golf outings, visits to the Ringling Museum, shopping on St. Armands Circle, sunset cruises, and group cooking in your rental's full kitchen.

How do you handle check-in for large groups?

We provide contactless self-check-in with keypad entry codes, so your group can arrive at any time without coordinating with a desk. Detailed arrival instructions, WiFi passwords, and a digital guidebook are sent before your trip. Our local team is available 24/7 if you need anything.
