Time to Commit to That Vacation

The best part about renting instead of buying? You don't haul it home. Book rentals for your full arrival day, not the day before—companies typically deliver morning to noon. We coordinate with local vendors, so you just show up and everything's ready.

Golf carts are absolutely the move on Anna Maria Island. Traffic on Bridge Street is brutal in peak season, parking is a nightmare, and golf carts are street-legal. You'll see them everywhere from November through April. Expect to pay $80–120/day for a rental, but you'll save that in parking and gas.

If you've got kids under 5, rent the cribs and high chairs. Checked luggage fees + car seat airlines hassle make rental the no-brainer. The only exception: if you're staying 10+ days, it might be cheaper to buy a basic pack-n-play from Walmart and leave it for the next guest.
