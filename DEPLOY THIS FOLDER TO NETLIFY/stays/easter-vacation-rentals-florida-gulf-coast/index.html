Easter week on Florida's Gulf Coast is peak spring break territory — warm water, sunny skies, and enough to do that nobody gets bored. Why the Gulf Coast for Easter

Florida's Gulf Coast — specifically the Bradenton, Anna Maria Island, and Sarasota corridor — has a different energy than the Atlantic side. The water is calmer (no strong currents), the sunsets are on the beach instead of behind you, and the pace is slower. For Easter gatherings, this matters. You want a place where the whole family can relax, not dodge jet skis.

Our properties are built for group holidays. The Oasis sleeps 16 with a massive pool, two hot tubs, and a backyard mini golf course — keep the kids entertained for hours. Dockside Dreams gives you waterfront dock access for fishing or just watching dolphins cruise past. Every home has a full gourmet kitchen, because Easter brunch tastes better when you're not fighting for a restaurant reservation.

Easter Week Activities Worth Your Time

Anna Maria Island hosts an annual Easter egg hunt at Bayfront Park — it's free, chaotic in the best way, and the kids talk about it for months. The Anna Maria Island area guide has the full rundown on island events. For a more structured outing, the Bishop Museum of Science and Nature in Bradenton runs a spring wildlife exhibit that's genuinely interesting for adults and kids alike.

On the water side, April is prime tarpon season. Charter boats out of Cortez Village and Longboat Key run half-day trips that are perfect for a father-son Easter morning. The Manatee River warms up enough by early April that kayaking through Robinson Preserve is comfortable — and you'll likely spot manatees, osprey, and the occasional eagle.

Weather and What to Pack

Early April on the Gulf Coast is the sweet spot. Expect daytime temps of 78-84°F, overnight lows around 65°F, and water temperatures that are swimmable without the "gasp-when-you-wade-in" moment. Rain is possible but brief — afternoon storms blow through in 20 minutes and leave behind the most dramatic sunsets you've ever seen.

Pack reef-safe sunscreen (Manatee County takes marine conservation seriously), a light jacket for evening boat rides, and water shoes if you plan to kayak through the mangroves. Our homes provide beach chairs, umbrellas, and coolers, so leave those at home.

Easter Rental Questions

When is Easter 2026?

Easter Sunday is April 5, 2026. Most families book vacation rentals for the full week of March 30 through April 6. Gulf Coast availability drops significantly by February for Easter week, so early booking is recommended.

How warm is the Gulf Coast during Easter week?

Early April brings daytime highs of 78-84°F and Gulf water temperatures around 76°F. The water is comfortably swimmable, and afternoon rain showers are brief (usually 15-20 minutes). It is one of the best times of year for beach weather on Florida's west coast.

Are there Easter events near Anna Maria Island?

Yes. Anna Maria Island hosts a free community Easter egg hunt at Bayfront Park. The Bishop Museum of Science and Nature in Bradenton runs spring wildlife programs. Many local churches host sunrise services on the beach. Check the Anna Maria Island Chamber website for the latest event listings.
