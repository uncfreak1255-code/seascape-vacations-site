Memorial Day Weekend Rentals — Florida Gulf Coast

Three-day weekend, heated pool, Gulf beaches 15 minutes away. Summer starts here.

Updated March 2026

By Seascape Vacations Team | Last Updated: March 2026

Memorial Day weekend marks the unofficial start of summer, and spending it at a Gulf Coast vacation rental is one of the smartest plays you can make. Late May weather in Bradenton is genuinely perfect — mid-80s, lower humidity than peak summer, and Gulf water that's warmed up enough (82-84°F) for comfortable swimming. You get summer vibes without the July heat or the spring break crowds.

Seascape Vacations' properties give you something a beach hotel can't: space to spread out, a private pool that's yours alone, and a full kitchen so you're not spending $200/day feeding the family at restaurants. For a three-day weekend, the value proposition is even stronger — you're paying per night, not per person, so bringing the whole crew keeps the cost reasonable.

Why Late May Is Actually the Best Beach Weather

Ask any local and they'll tell you: late May is the sweet spot on the Gulf Coast calendar. Here's why it beats the typical summer weeks:

Making the Most of a 3-Day Weekend

Friday evening: Arrive, unpack, jump in the pool. Grab takeout from Anna Maria Oyster Bar or Swordfish Grill on your way in — you're on vacation, nobody's cooking the first night. The sunset over the Manatee River from our waterfront properties hits around 8:15 PM in late May, and it's worth sitting on the dock for.

Saturday: Beach day. Coquina Beach on Anna Maria Island has the best sand and natural shade from the Australian pines. Pack the cooler (Publix rotisserie chicken, fruit, drinks), rent an umbrella from one of the beach vendors, and camp out for 4-5 hours. Back to the rental for pool time. Evening: grill at the house or walk the Riverwalk in downtown Bradenton.

Sunday: Activity day. Rent a pontoon from Bradenton Beach Marina, kayak through Robinson Preserve, or do a dolphin tour from Cortez. Afternoon: pool and lanai time. This is the day most guests say they wished they'd booked a longer trip.

Monday: Slow morning. Coffee on the lanai, one last pool dip, and a late checkout if available (ask when booking — we can often accommodate noon checkout on slower weekends). Hit the road before lunch traffic.

Memorial Day Events Around Bradenton

Bradenton and the surrounding area take Memorial Day seriously. The Manatee County Veterans Memorial ceremony (free, starts at 10 AM) is a moving tribute with speakers, wreath-laying, and a flyover. It's at Riverview Memorial Park, a 10-minute drive from our properties. Several restaurants and businesses run Memorial Day weekend specials — check the Bradenton Herald events calendar that week.

Anna Maria Island typically hosts a casual beach parade and sandcastle building competition on the holiday itself. Holmes Beach runs a community cookout near the volleyball courts. Nothing massive or formal — just the kind of small-town celebration that makes island life charming.

Booking Tip: Memorial Day weekend is a 3-night minimum at most properties. The best strategy is to arrive Thursday evening (4 nights instead of 3) — you get an extra day at a lower per-night rate, and you skip the Friday traffic that builds on I-75 southbound from Tampa.

A Memorial Day weekend at a Gulf Coast hotel runs $200-300/night for a standard room. For a family of 4-6, you need either a suite ($400-500/night) or two rooms ($400-600/night combined). Three nights: $1,200-1,800 for the hotel alone, plus $150-250/day eating out because hotel kitchenettes barely function.

Split between two families: $525 each for the whole weekend. The savings on dining alone (cooking 6 of 9 meals at home) covers groceries and then some. Common Questions

Memorial Day Weekend FAQ

What's the minimum stay for Memorial Day weekend?

Most Seascape properties require a 3-night minimum over Memorial Day weekend. We recommend booking 4 nights (Thursday-Monday) for a better per-night rate and to avoid Friday afternoon traffic on I-75 southbound.

Is the pool warm enough in late May?

Yes — all pools are heated to 82-86°F year-round, and late May water temperatures are naturally warm. The Gulf of Mexico averages 82-84°F by Memorial Day weekend, comfortable for extended swimming without a wetsuit.

What's the weather like Memorial Day weekend?

Late May in Bradenton is ideal: highs of 83-87°F, moderate humidity (lower than peak summer), and only 3-4 rain days per month. The Gulf water is warm, breezes are steady, and afternoon thunderstorms haven't hit their summer peak yet.

Are beaches crowded on Memorial Day?

Moderate crowds — less than peak summer but busier than a normal weekday. The best strategy is to arrive at the beach by 9 AM to secure a good spot. Coquina Beach and Bean Point on Anna Maria Island tend to spread out better than the smaller Holmes Beach access points.

How far in advance should I book?

Memorial Day weekend properties typically book 4-6 weeks ahead. We recommend booking by mid-April for the best selection. Earlier booking also locks in shoulder-season pricing before summer rates take effect in June.
