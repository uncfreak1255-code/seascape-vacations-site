Large Group Vacation Rentals Anna Maria Island — Up to 16 Guests

Updated March 2026

What to Know Upfront

Anna Maria Island has fewer truly large homes than most people expect. The island is 7 miles long and zoned to keep it residential — which keeps it quiet and uncrowded, but also means that homes sleeping 12–16 guests are rare. Seascape Vacations manages two of the largest privately available homes on AMI: The Oasis (sleeps 16, 6 bedrooms, heated pool) and Dockside Dreams (sleeps 12, 5 bedrooms, private dock). Both are available for direct booking. Both include full kitchens, high-speed WiFi, and outdoor spaces designed for groups. Rates run $595–$950/night depending on season. Split across 12 guests, that's $50–$80/person/night — with a private pool included.

The Oasis — Anna Maria Island's Largest Rental Home

Most visitors to Anna Maria Island come for the same reason the Hendersons from Chicago did last spring: they'd vacationed here with two kids, loved it, and now wanted to bring both sets of grandparents, a sibling family, and a college-age nephew. That's 14 people. The island's rental inventory shrank every time they clicked "max guests: 14" on Vrbo. What came back was two properties three miles apart, or inflated rates for homes that technically slept 14 but put four adults in a pull-out couch situation.

The Oasis was designed for exactly this. Six real bedrooms — four queens and two kings — each with private or semi-private bath access. A screened heated pool with a covered lanai big enough for 14 to eat dinner together. An outdoor kitchen with a built-in grill. The house is 12 minutes on foot from Manatee Beach, sits on a quiet residential street, and has driveway parking for 4 vehicles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the largest vacation rental available on Anna Maria Island?

Seascape's The Oasis sleeps 16 guests across 6 bedrooms — one of the largest privately managed homes on AMI. It includes a heated screened pool, outdoor kitchen, and is 12 minutes from Manatee Beach. Most Anna Maria Island large homes top out at 10–12 guests; 16-guest capacity is uncommon on this island due to residential zoning and lot sizes.

How much does a large group rental on Anna Maria Island cost per night?

Expect $595–$950/night for homes sleeping 12–16 guests on Anna Maria Island. Split across 12 people, that's roughly $50–$80/person/night — comparable to two hotel rooms but with a private pool, full kitchen, and no restaurant bills for breakfast. Peak season (December–April) and spring break weeks command the higher end.

Do Anna Maria Island large group rentals allow events or parties?

AMI's short-term rental ordinance prohibits amplified outdoor music after 10pm and limits gatherings to registered guests. Quiet celebrations of registered guests — birthdays, reunions, anniversaries — are welcome at Seascape properties. Weddings or events with outside guests require permits not available at residential rentals. We discuss group plans upfront and confirm the rental is a good fit before accepting the booking.

Is it better to book one large AMI home vs. two smaller homes?

One large home wins for groups of 10–16. You share one kitchen, one pool, one gathering space, and pay less in total. Two separate AMI properties typically costs $100–$250 more per night combined, splits your group's social core, and often puts one subgroup farther from the beach.
