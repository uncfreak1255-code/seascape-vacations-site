  • The Oasis: Sleeps 16 guests (6 bedrooms, screened heated pool, outdoor kitchen)
  • Dockside Dreams: Sleeps 12 guests (5 bedrooms, private dock, waterfront)

Rates: $595–$950 per night, or $50–$80 per person per night—30–40% cheaper than booking separate Airbnb or Vrbo units.

Best for: Family reunions, milestone celebrations, girls'/guys' getaways, and destination team retreats.

One Large Home vs. Two Smaller Homes

Groups debating between renting one large home or splitting into two properties often ask: Which is better? The answer depends on your group dynamics, budget, and what you value most. Here's how The Oasis stacks up:

Factor One Large Home Two Smaller Homes
Total Cost $895/night (The Oasis) $1,200–$1,400/night (2 homes)
Cost per Person (14 guests) $64/person $85–$100/person
Shared Meals ✓ Easy (one kitchen) ✗ Requires coordination
Single Pool ✓ Everyone together ✗ Split time/logistics
Beach Logistics ✓ One meeting point ✗ Two groups, two spots
Booking Complexity ✓ One booking, one contract ✗ Two bookings, two rules

The verdict: Choose one large home if your group wants to bond, share meals, and keep costs down. Choose two homes if your group values privacy, has families with different schedules, or includes people who prefer separation. For most family reunions and friend getaways, one home wins on cost and connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the largest vacation rental available on Anna Maria Island?

The Oasis is Anna Maria Island's largest private vacation rental, sleeping up to 16 guests. It features 6 bedrooms, 5.5 bathrooms, a screened heated pool, outdoor kitchen, and waterfront views. Seascape Vacations also offers Dockside Dreams, which sleeps 12 guests with 5 bedrooms, private dock, and waterfront amenities. Both homes are fully managed by Seascape, meaning consistent quality, responsive management, and direct communication with the owners.

How much do large group homes cost per night on Anna Maria Island?

Seascape Vacations' large group homes range from $595–$950 per night depending on the property, season, and occupancy. Per-person rates work out to $50–$80 per person per night, which is 30–40% cheaper than booking multiple Airbnb or Vrbo properties separately. Off-season (June–August, September–October) rates are lower, while peak season (December–January, spring break) commands higher rates. Seascape also offers modest discounts for bookings of 14+ nights or repeat guests.

Do you allow events or parties in large group homes?

Seascape Vacations' large group homes are designed for family reunions, milestone celebrations (birthdays, anniversaries, retirements), and group vacations. Events and gatherings are welcome, and the homes have ample space for group activities. However, large parties with public promotion, excessive noise after 10 PM, or damage risk are not permitted. External catering, live entertainment, and evening events are allowed with prior notice. Please contact Seascape Vacations directly about your specific event to confirm details and any special requirements.

Is it better to rent one large home or two smaller homes?

It depends on your group dynamics. One large home (like The Oasis) is best for groups wanting to share meals, pool time, and activities—plus it's simpler to manage one booking. Two smaller homes work better for groups that prefer separate spaces, families with young children who need quiet, or groups wanting more privacy.
