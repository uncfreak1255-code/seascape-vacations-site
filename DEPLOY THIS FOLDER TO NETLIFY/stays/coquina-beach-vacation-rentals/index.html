About Stay Near Coquina Beach AMI

Why Coquina Beach is a Local Favorite

While visitors crowd the northern beaches, locals head to Coquina Beach on the southern end of Anna Maria Island. Free parking, clean facilities, and a laid-back vibe make it the perfect home base for your Gulf Coast vacation.

Dog-Friendly Paradise: Unlike most Florida beaches, Coquina Beach welcomes dogs! The southern portion allows leashed dogs, making it one of the few beaches where your furry family member can enjoy the waves.

Shelling & Nature: The currents at Coquina deposit shells from across the Gulf. Early morning low tides reveal sand dollars, fighting conchs, and beautiful scallop shells. The adjacent Leffis Key preserve offers kayaking and nature trails.

Perfect Location: Our Bradenton vacation rentals are just 5-10 minutes from Coquina Beach via the Cortez Bridge. Enjoy private pools at home and beach days at Coquina—the best of both worlds.
