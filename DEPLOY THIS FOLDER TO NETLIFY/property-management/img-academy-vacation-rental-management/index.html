The IMG Academy Rental Opportunity

IMG Academy attracts over 12,000 student-athletes annually, plus countless families for camps, tournaments, and recruitment visits. This creates consistent year-round demand for quality vacation rentals—something hotels can't match.

Why Sports Families Prefer Vacation Rentals: Traveling sports families need space for equipment, room for meal preparation, and pools for recovery. They often travel in groups of 2-4 families sharing costs. Your 4-5 bedroom home is exactly what they're looking for.

Our Specialized Marketing: We actively market to the IMG Academy community, tennis families, youth sports tournaments, and college recruitment visitors. Our SEO strategy specifically targets these high-value guests who book 5-14 night stays.

Premium Rates: Sports families are willing to pay premium rates for the right amenities and proximity. Heated pools, multiple bedrooms, and full kitchens command top dollar from this demographic.

Why Sports Families Choose Vacation Rentals

Hotel rooms simply don't work for traveling sports families. Multiple families sharing costs need kitchen access for meal prep, laundry facilities for gear, and gathering space for team bonding. Your 4-5 bedroom home with pool is exactly what they're searching for on Airbnb and VRBO.

These guests also book longer stays—5 to 14 nights is typical for tournament season. Extended stays mean higher total revenue and lower turnover costs. Unlike leisure travelers who book weekends, sports families fill weekday slots you'd otherwise struggle to fill.

The best part? They're less price-sensitive. When your child is competing in a tournament, the nightly rate matters less than the convenience and comfort. Parents will pay premium rates for the right property in the right location.
