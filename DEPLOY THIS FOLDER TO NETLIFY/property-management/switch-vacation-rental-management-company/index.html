More About Ready to Switch Property Managers?

Signs It's Time to Switch

Many owners stay with underperforming managers because switching seems complicated. If you're experiencing these issues, it's time to explore options:

  • Declining bookings or revenue without explanation
  • Poor guest reviews affecting your property's reputation
  • Communication gaps—you can't reach your manager
  • Hidden fees eating into your returns
  • Maintenance issues going unaddressed
  • Outdated pricing leaving money on the table

Our Switching Checklist:

  1. Review Your Contract: Most management agreements require 30-90 days notice. We'll help you understand your obligations.
  2. Secure Your Assets: Ensure you have copies of all photos, reviews, and guest data. These belong to you.
  3. Current Bookings: We honor all existing reservations and handle guest communication simplely.
  4. Platform Transfers: We transfer or recreate your Airbnb, VRBO, and other listings—preserving review history when possible.
  5. Property Inspection: We conduct a thorough property assessment and address any deferred maintenance.
  6. Fresh Photography: Professional photos are included to showcase your property's best features.

We Make Switching Property Managers Simple

Worried about downtime during the transition? We handle it. We coordinate directly with your current manager, ensure no bookings are lost, and we're transparent about every step. Your guests never notice management changed — which is exactly how it should be.

Many owners tell us they didn't switch sooner because they feared the transition. After working with us, they realize the risk of staying with an underperforming manager is far higher than the short-term coordination effort of switching. The average switch takes 2-3 weeks from first call to full handoff. During that time, your calendar stays active, and we're communicating with your existing platform providers to ensure continuity.

Your investment property shouldn't feel like a headache. If your current manager isn't delivering strong annual returns, increasing occupancy month over month, or treating your guests with genuine care — let's talk. The conversation is free, and it might be the best decision you make this year.
