Frequently Asked Questions

Switch from Self-Managing to Professional — FAQ

Will I lose my Airbnb Superhost status when switching to professional management? +
No. Your Airbnb account, ratings, and Superhost status transfer completely to your property manager. We manage your Airbnb account on your behalf while maintaining all account security and status. Most properties maintain or improve their Superhost status under professional management because we maintain the high standards that earned it in the first place and add professional systems that ensure consistent excellence.
How much will my rental income increase by switching to professional management? +
Experienced self-managers typically see 15-30% income increases within 6-12 months of switching to professional management. This comes from optimized dynamic pricing, reduced vacancy periods, enhanced guest experience improving reviews and repeat bookings, and expanded distribution to additional platforms like VRBO and Booking.com. Management fees (typically 28-32%) are more than offset by the increased revenue.
What happens to my Airbnb calendar and bookings when transitioning to a property manager? +
Your Airbnb account, existing bookings, and calendar integrate easyly into our Hostaway PMS system, which synchronizes across all platforms. No guest bookings are lost or disrupted during the transition. We take over account management, continue honoring existing reservations, and implement our optimized pricing and operational systems going forward. The transition is transparent to your guests.
Will I still be able to access my Airbnb account and monitor my property? +
Yes. We recommend property owners maintain account access so you can view your property's performance, read reviews, and monitor overall status. However, you delegate day-to-day management (booking acceptance, guest communication, pricing adjustments) to our team. This gives you visibility while eliminating the operational burden.
How does professional management improve Airbnb pricing versus self-management? +
Professional managers use sophisticated dynamic pricing tools that analyze historical booking data, market demand, competitive pricing, and local events to optimize rates algorithmically. Most self-managers adjust pricing reactively or use Airbnb's automatic pricing, which often under-prices properties. Our data-driven approach typically increases average nightly rates 8-15% while maintaining or improving occupancy rates.
What's the transition process like when switching to professional management? +
Our onboarding process is straightforward: we meet to discuss your property, goals, and current performance; we review all existing bookings and property details; we discuss pricing and operational strategy; and we take over management activities. For Airbnb properties, the transition typically takes 1-2 weeks. We maintain all continuity with guests, honor existing bookings, and implement our optimized systems going forward. Most owners report a smooth, stress-free transition.
