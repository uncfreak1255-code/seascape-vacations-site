More About How Does Your Rental Stack Up?

Data-Driven Property Analysis

Every property owner wonders: Am I leaving money on the table? Our performance comparison uses real market data to benchmark your property against comparable rentals.

What We Analyze:

  • Occupancy Rate: How your booked nights compare to market averages
  • Average Daily Rate (ADR): Are you pricing competitively?
  • Revenue Per Available Night (RevPAN): The true measure of performance
  • Seasonal Patterns: Identifying missed opportunities
  • Booking Lead Time: When guests book similar properties

Actionable Insights: We don't just show you data—we explain what it means and how to improve. If you're self-managing or considering professional management, understanding your benchmarks is essential.

What the Numbers Really Tell You

RevPAN is the metric that matters. Occupancy alone is misleading—you could be 80% booked at discount rates and still underperform a property at 65% occupancy charging premium rates. RevPAN captures the full picture of profitability.

If your property is underperforming benchmarks, the fix usually isn't market-related. Pricing strategy, photography, guest experience, and reviews drive your results. Professional management focuses on these controllable factors to improve your bottom line.

Many self-managing owners don't track these metrics at all. They leave money on the table by pricing reactively instead of strategically. We benchmark your property monthly and adjust pricing, marketing, and amenities to maximize your specific revenue potential.
