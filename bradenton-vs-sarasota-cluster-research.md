## Recommended Build Order

### Priority 1: High-impact, low-competition (Build first)

**1. `/guides/bradenton-vs-sarasota-families/`**
- **Why first:** Families are the #1 vacation rental audience. No dedicated comparison page exists. The PAA "Is Bradenton or Sarasota better for a family vacation?" has no direct answer in the SERPs.
- **Content angle:** Schools (Sarasota 18 A-rated vs Bradenton 3), kid-friendly restaurants, beach safety (AMI has calmer water), playgrounds/parks, kid activities (LECOM Park, Mote Marine, Jungle Gardens), vacation rental suitability (pool homes for families)
- **Internal links FROM:** pillar page, AMI vs Siesta Key families page, family vacation rental stays pages
- **Internal links TO:** family property pages (The Oasis, Bradenton Pool Home), Anna Maria guides

**2. `/guides/bradenton-vs-sarasota-beaches/`**
- **Why second:** "Which has better beaches" is the #1 decision factor for vacationers. The pillar page covers this lightly but a deep-dive with comparison tables (sand quality, crowd levels, parking, amenities, water clarity) would be a GEO magnet.
- **Content angle:** Not just AMI vs Siesta Key (we have that page) — include mainland beaches, beach bars, sunset spots, water sports access, beach accessibility, shelling vs surfing
- **Internal links FROM:** pillar page, all beach-related stays pages
- **Internal links TO:** AMI vs Siesta Key, property pages near each beach

**3. `/guides/bradenton-vs-sarasota-restaurants/`**
- **Why third:** Zero dedicated comparison pages in the SERPs — pure first-mover advantage. Restaurant quality directly influences vacation destination choice. Hyper-local content that AI engines love (specific restaurant names, dishes, prices).
- **Content angle:** Waterfront dining, seafood spots, kid-friendly restaurants, date night, breakfast/brunch, budget vs splurge, neighborhood food scenes (downtown SRQ vs Village of the Arts vs Bridge Street)
- **Internal links FROM:** pillar page, all area guides
- **Internal links TO:** property pages (mention nearby restaurants for each)

### Priority 2: Valuable but smaller audience (Build next month)

**4. `/guides/bradenton-vs-sarasota-retirement/`**
- **Why:** Retirees are a secondary vacation rental audience — they book long-term stays (4-8 weeks) to "test" an area before moving. Only one competitor (discoveryvillages.com) has this page.
- **Content angle:** Healthcare access, 55+ communities, golf courses, cost of living on fixed income, social scene, proximity to airports, walkability

**5. `/guides/bradenton-vs-sarasota-cost-of-living/`**
- **Why:** Attracts snowbirds and "try before you buy" relocators. High search volume but bestplaces.net dominates with raw data. Our angle: *what these costs mean for vacationers and seasonal visitors* (rental prices, dining costs, grocery costs, entertainment costs).
- **Content angle:** Frame as "vacation cost comparison" — average nightly rental rate, meal costs, activity costs, transportation. Include a "1-week vacation budget" comparison table.

### Priority 3: Cluster maintenance

**6. Add FAQ schema to pillar page for these PAA questions:**
- Is Bradenton or Sarasota better for a family vacation? (link to families page)
- Is Bradenton cheaper than Sarasota? (link to cost page)
- Which has better beaches, Bradenton or Sarasota? (link to beaches page)
- Is Bradenton or Sarasota better for retirement? (link to retirement page)

**7. Strengthen internal links between all cluster pages** — every new page should link to 3-4 other cluster pages + the pillar, and receive links back from 3-4 existing pages.
